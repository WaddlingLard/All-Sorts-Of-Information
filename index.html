What is this website?

Hello, if you've stumbled on to this website, welcome! This is All Sorts of Information. It is a website that mimics the same behaviors of a Wikipedia/Reddit hybrid similar to Fandom. It is a repository of information from a wide collection of articles sourced by people who want to source their pools of knowledge/research (hopefully).

Start by looking around, there are already several articles stored and you can take a look. This website is currently a work in progress and there are many things to do. In fact, I can just have a TO-DO sheet right here saying what I'm working on.

What can I do?

You can take a look at the variety of articles, but you can also create your own pages. There is an article creator page where you can name, provide content, and even list an image and provide attributes for the topic. On the article pages you can take part in discussions by posting your own or commenting on other peer's posts!

This is the main goal of the website! It is the lifeblood for users to share their knowledge and interact with posts to create a sense of community. One user has something that another does not and taking part in the conversation allows everyone to be on the same page.

So I recommend to take part in the website. Add what you feel is needed. There are many topics to be stored and discussed.
